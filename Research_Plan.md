Research Plan

Objective

The objective of this project is to experimentally investigate whether nanosecond laser ablation of PTFE can generate measurable thrust suitable for future CubeSat propulsion systems.

Scientific Hypothesis

Nanosecond laser pulses with energies between 10 mJ and 100 mJ will produce a directional plasma plume capable of generating measurable thrust under vacuum conditions.

Experimental Procedure

1. Prepare PTFE samples.
2. Place the target inside a vacuum chamber.
3. Irradiate the target using nanosecond laser pulses.
4. Measure generated thrust.
5. Measure propellant mass loss.
6. Record plasma plume characteristics.
7. Compare measurements with theoretical predictions.

Measurements

- Thrust
- Impulse
- Specific impulse (Isp)
- Mass loss
- Plasma plume geometry
- Repeatability

Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if measurable thrust is generated and the experimental data are consistent with theoretical expectations.
