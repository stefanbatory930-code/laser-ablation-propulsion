Calculations

Objective

The purpose of these calculations is to establish the theoretical framework for analyzing the experimental results.

Laser Parameters

- Pulse energy: 10–100 mJ
- Pulse duration: Nanoseconds
- Propellant: PTFE

Measured Quantities

The following quantities will be measured experimentally:

- Thrust (N)
- Impulse per pulse (N·s)
- Ablated propellant mass (kg)
- Repetition rate (Hz)

Specific Impulse

Specific impulse will be calculated using:

Isp = Impulse / (Mass × g₀)

where:

- Impulse = measured impulse per pulse
- Mass = ablated propellant mass
- g₀ = 9.80665 m/s²

Average Thrust

Average thrust will be determined from the measured force during continuous laser operation.

Experimental Validation

No propulsion performance is assumed in advance. All conclusions will be based exclusively on measured experimental data obtained during vacuum testing.
